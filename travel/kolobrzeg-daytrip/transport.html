Transport

By car — Pobierowo to Kołobrzeg

Distance: 59 km, ~1 h 9 min
driving route in Google Maps
Steps: East on DW102 via Trzęsacz/Rewal and Trzebiatów, then DW109 through Mrzeżyno toward Kołobrzeg and into the old town. The only one of the five trips that heads east.
Estimated time: ~1 h 9 min
Notes: Park near the old town (town hall/cathedral) or by the seaside park. Old town and seaside are ~1.5 km apart — a pleasant walk, or move the car once.

By car — Kołobrzeg to Pobierowo

Distance: 59 km, ~1 h 10 min
driving route in Google Maps
Steps: Back via DW109/DW102 west through Trzebiatów and the Rewal coast to Pobierowo.
Estimated time: ~1 h 10 min

Public transport

Distance: -
Steps: A regional/PKS bus connection exists along the coast but is slow and infrequent; there's no convenient direct link from Pobierowo. Driving is recommended.
Operating hours: -
Notes: Within Kołobrzeg a seasonal tourist mini-train and city buses link the old town with the seaside if you'd rather not walk the 1.5 km.

Parking & Tips

Notes: Do the old town first (arms museum, cathedral, town hall — all clustered), then head to the seaside for the lighthouse, pier, a pirate cruise and the spa park/beach. Arms Museum opens at 9:00 — a good early start before the beach.
