Attractions

Stary Rynek & Town Hall Goats (Koziołki)

Website: https://mnp.art.pl/
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Stary+Rynek+Ratusz+Poznan
Open: Old Market Square open 24/7. Mechanical billy-goats butt heads daily at 12:00 noon, preceded by a bugle call (hejnał). The Town Hall interior (Museum of Poznań History) is under long-term renovation, but the goats mechanism on the tower runs every day.
Walk: 1.3 km
route in Google Maps
Walk steps: Head north up the Półwiejska pedestrian shopping street, then via plac Wiosny Ludów and Szkolna into the Old Market Square — about 18 min.
Public transit: 2.5 km, ~17 min
route in Google Maps
Transit steps: Walk ~0.4 km to the Półwiejska stop, take TRAM 7 four stops to Marcinkowskiego, then walk ~0.5 km to the square.
Tickets: Free to watch the goats and explore the square. Time it for 11:50 to get a good spot before noon.

Rogalowe Muzeum Poznania (Croissant Museum)

Website: https://rogalowemuzeum.pl/en/
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Rogalowe+Muzeum+Poznania+Klasztorna+23+Poznan
Open: Interactive shows daily; English shows about 11:00, 12:30, 14:00, 15:30 (a 17:00 show is added on Saturdays and in summer). Arrive 10 min early to validate your ticket. Entrance through the courtyard at Klasztorna 23 (Stary Rynek 41), 1st floor.
Walk: 1.4 km
route in Google Maps
Walk steps: Same route as the Old Market Square — up Półwiejska and Szkolna to Stary Rynek; the entrance is just off the square on Klasztorna.
Public transit: 2.5 km, ~18 min
route in Google Maps
Transit steps: Walk ~0.4 km to Półwiejska, take TRAM 7 four stops to Marcinkowskiego, then walk ~0.5 km to the museum entrance on Klasztorna.
Tickets: ~47 PLN per person for the English show; children take part in kneading the dough. Small children may pay less — confirm at the box office. Book online at rogalowemuzeum.pl or call +48 690 077 800.

Nowe Zoo (New Zoo)

Website: https://zoo.poznan.pl/
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Nowe+Zoo+Poznan+Krancowa+81
Open: Daily 9:00–19:00 (last entry 18:00). A free trackless train runs inside the zoo May–August, handy with a stroller.
Walk: 6.0 km — too far on foot; use transit or the Maltanka railway.
route in Google Maps
Public transit: 7.9 km, ~36 min
route in Google Maps
Transit steps: Walk ~0.4 km to Półwiejska, take TRAM 6 seven stops to Baraniaka, change to BUS 184 six stops to the Nowe Zoo stop (one transfer). Fun alternative: ride the Maltanka railway to the "Zwierzyniec" stop by the main gate.
Tickets: Normal 45 PLN, reduced 30 PLN, family ticket 80 PLN (2 adults + children, up to 6 people); under 3 free. Buy online at bilety.zoo.poznan.pl or at the gate.

Maltanka Railway & Lake Malta

Website: https://www.mpk.poznan.pl/en/maltanka-park-railway/
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Kolejka+Parkowa+Maltanka+Poznan
Open: Seasonal narrow-gauge railway (approx. late March–October). Weekends/holidays: departures every 30 min, ~10:00–18:30 from Maltanka station, ~10:30–19:00 from Zwierzyniec. The lakeside promenade, playgrounds and pedal-boats are open year-round.
Walk: 4.6 km — too far; use transit.
route in Google Maps
Public transit: 5.3 km, ~29 min
route in Google Maps
Transit steps: Walk ~0.4 km to Półwiejska, take TRAM 6 to the Lake Malta area (Termalna), then a short lakeside walk. The line runs Rondo Śródka ↔ Zwierzyniec along the north shore of the lake.
Tickets: One-way: 15 PLN normal, 10 PLN child under 16; family ticket (max 5, up to 2 adults) 45 PLN. Buy at the platform before departure.

route in Google Maps

route in Google Maps
