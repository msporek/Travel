Transport

AIRPORT TO HOTEL

Metro / Train

Distance: -
Steps: There is NO rail or metro link to Poznań–Ławica. Use bus 159 or a taxi (below).
Ticket: -
Operating hours: -
Notes: The airport is only ~7–8 km from the centre, so the bus or a taxi are the two real options.

Bus

Distance: 8.1 km, ~39 min total (bus + walk)
transit route in Google Maps
Steps: Take BUS 159 from the stop right outside arrivals to Poznań Główny (main station) — about 20–25 min — then walk ~1.1 km, or hop a tram for the last stretch to the hotel.
Ticket: 45-min ticket: 7 PLN normal / 3.50 PLN reduced (child up to 16). Children under 7 ride free. Family of four ≈ 17.50 PLN. Buy from the machine at the stop or in the terminal (mobile apps for card payment) and validate on board.
Operating hours: Bus 159 runs ~04:30–24:00, every 15–25 min. Night line 242 covers late arrivals.
Notes: Express "Line L" also runs airport ↔ Rondo Kaponiera if that suits you better.

Taxi / Rideshare

Distance: 7.6 km, ~21 min driving
driving route in Google Maps
Apps available: Uber: yes | Bolt: yes | FreeNow: yes | Official airport taxi: yes
Estimated fare: Bolt EUR ~7–12 (PLN 30–50) | Uber PLN 30–50 | Official taxi PLN 30–50 (up to ~60 at night)
Estimated time: off-peak ~20 min, peak ~30 min
Notes: With two kids and luggage this is the easiest arrival option — one car for all four. Order in the app from the terminal; the official taxi rank is just outside arrivals.

Ticket: -
Operating hours: -

Bus

Distance: 7.2 km, ~35 min total
transit route in Google Maps
Steps: Walk ~0.4 km to Wierzbięcice, take TRAM 8 three stops to Bałtyk, then BUS 159 to Ławica Airport (one transfer, ~35 min). Alternatively walk to Poznań Główny and take bus 159 direct.
Ticket: A 45-min ticket (7 / 3.50 PLN) covers a tram+bus transfer if done within the time window; otherwise buy a 90-min ticket (9 / 4.50 PLN). Children under 7 free; 13-yo reduced.
Operating hours: ~04:30–24:00, every 15–25 min.
Notes: Allow a buffer for the transfer; for an early flight a taxi is safer.

Transit Card Quick Guide

System: ZTM Poznań runs trams + buses on the same tickets. Tourists don't need a smart-card — just buy single time-tickets.
Single tickets: 15-min 5 / 2.50 PLN · 45-min 7 / 3.50 PLN · 90-min 9 / 4.50 PLN (normal / reduced). Time-based, so any transfer within the window is free.
Children: Free until 30 September of the year a child turns 7 — your 4-year-old rides FREE. Reduced fare for children up to 16 — your 13-year-old pays reduced (carry ID/age proof).
Where to buy: Yellow machines at stops and on board (newer ones take cards), kiosks (Ruch/Żabka), or apps: mPay, moBILET, CallPay, jakdojade.
Do / Don't: DO validate the moment you board. DON'T ride unvalidated — inspectors check and fine. A 45-min ticket covers the airport bus or most single hops across the centre.
EN: Bus 159 links Ławica airport with the main station and city centre; a single 45-min ticket is enough for the ride.
PL: Autobus 159 łączy lotnisko Ławica z dworcem głównym i centrum; na przejazd wystarczy jeden bilet 45-minutowy. Pamiętajcie o skasowaniu biletu po wejściu.
