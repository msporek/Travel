Transport

AIRPORT TO HOTEL

Flybussen (Airport Express Coach)

Distance: 14 km, ~25-30 min
Transit on Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Stavanger%20lufthavn%20Sola&destination=Scandic%20Royal%20Stavanger%2C%20Lokkeveien%2026%2C%204002%20Stavanger&travelmode=transit
Steps: Exit arrivals, walk 2 min to Flybussen stop directly outside terminal. Take Flybussen toward Stavanger Sentrum (every 15-20 min). Get off at "Mosvatnet" stop (closest to hotel - check with driver) or "Stavanger Sentrum" terminus. From Mosvatnet ~10 min walk to hotel; from Sentrum ~5 min walk.
Ticket: Single NOK 179 | Return (within 30 days) NOK 264 | Children under 4 free | Children 4-17 50% off. Buy online at flybussen.no, at airport vending machines, or from driver.
Operating hours: ~05:00-00:30 daily, every 15-20 min
Notes: Fastest direct option to Stavanger Sentrum. Comfortable coach with luggage racks + WiFi. Best balance of cost vs. speed for a family of 4 with luggage.

Kolumbus Bus (Bus 42)

Distance: 16.1 km, ~23 min
transit route in Google Maps
Steps: Walk to Sola Lufthavn bus stop, take Kolumbus Bus 42 toward Sandnes. Get off at Sande terrasse (12 min), then walk + transfer to bus toward Stavanger sentrum, OR get a taxi from Sande terrasse. Walking the last leg from a closer stop is also possible.
Ticket: Adult NOK 45 (1-hour transfer ticket) | Children under 4 free, 4-17 half-price. Buy via Kolumbus app, ticket vending machines, or from driver. 24-hour pass NOK 100.
Operating hours: ~05:30-00:30 daily
Notes: Cheaper than Flybussen (NOK 45 vs 179) but slower and requires a transfer. Worth it if you have minimal luggage and time. The Kolumbus app (download before arrival) is the easiest way to buy.

Taxi / Rideshare

Distance: 14.2 km, ~15 min driving (no traffic)
driving route in Google Maps
Apps available: Uber: limited service in Stavanger | Bolt: not available | FreeNow: not available | Norgestaxi: yes | Stavanger Taxi: yes
Estimated fare: Norgestaxi/Stavanger Taxi NOK 450-650 (peak/off-peak metered) | Uber when available NOK 400-600
Estimated time: 15 min off-peak, 25-35 min during morning rush
Notes: Norway is expensive for taxis. For a family of 4 + luggage, taxi is the fastest door-to-door option but costs ~3-4x Flybussen. Official taxi rank directly outside arrivals. Best to pre-order via Norgestaxi app or hotel concierge. Stavanger does not have heavy rideshare adoption - rely on traditional taxis.

HOTEL TO AIRPORT

Flybussen (Airport Express Coach)

Distance: 14 km, ~25-30 min
Transit on Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Scandic%20Royal%20Stavanger%2C%20Lokkeveien%2026%2C%204002%20Stavanger&destination=Stavanger%20lufthavn%20Sola%2C%204055%20Sola%2C%20Norway&travelmode=transit
Steps: Walk 5 min to "Mosvatnet" or "Stavanger Sentrum" Flybussen stop. Take Flybussen toward Sola Airport. ~25 min ride.
Ticket: Same as airport-to-hotel: Single NOK 179.
Operating hours: ~05:00-00:30 daily, every 15-20 min
Notes: For a Thursday Aug 6 departure, allow ~2 hours before international flight: 5 min walk + 25 min Flybussen + 90 min security/boarding margin = 2 hours. Hotel reception can advise on the closest Flybussen stop.

Kolumbus Bus

Distance: 23.2 km, ~46 min
transit route in Google Maps
Steps: Walk to Vaktapoteket stop, take bus toward Sandnes/Madla, transfer somewhere along the way to bus 42 (which serves the airport). 1 transfer required. Slower than Flybussen by ~20 min.
Ticket: Adult NOK 45 single. Children half price.
Operating hours: ~05:30-00:30 daily
Notes: Cheaper than Flybussen but with 1 transfer and longer total travel time. Skip if you are traveling with luggage and kids.

Taxi / Rideshare

Distance: 13.9 km, ~15 min driving
driving route in Google Maps
Apps available: Uber: limited | Norgestaxi: yes | Stavanger Taxi: yes | Hotel can call
Estimated fare: NOK 450-650
Estimated time: 15 min off-peak, 25-35 min in morning rush (07:30-09:30)
Notes: Hotel reception will call a taxi for you (no fee). Pre-order at least 30 min before pickup. For an early morning flight, lock in the taxi the night before. Smooth drop-off zone at Sola departures.
