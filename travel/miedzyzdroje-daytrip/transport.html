Transport

By car — Pobierowo to Międzyzdroje

Distance: 41 km, ~47 min
driving route in Google Maps
Steps: The coast road DW102 the whole way — through Dziwnów, across the Dziwna and onto Wolin island into Międzyzdroje.
Estimated time: ~47 min
Notes: Park near the centre/promenade (paid in summer; lots fill up by late morning — arrive early).

By car — Międzyzdroje to Pobierowo

Distance: 42 km, ~48 min
driving route in Google Maps
Steps: Back along DW102 via Wolin and Dziwnów to the Pobierowo coast.
Estimated time: ~48 min

Public transport

Distance: -
Steps: Międzyzdroje has a rail station on the Szczecin–Świnoujście line, but there is no convenient train/bus link from Pobierowo (you would first need to reach Wolin or Kamień). Driving is by far the simplest.
Operating hours: -
Notes: Inside town everything is walkable from the promenade; only the Bison Reserve and Turquoise Lake need the car again.

Parking & Tips

Notes: Międzyzdroje gets very busy in July–August; arrive before 10:00 for parking and to see the Bison Reserve before the crowds (and remember it's shut on Mondays). One central parking spot covers the pier, promenade, beach and museums.
