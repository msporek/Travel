Attractions

Międzyzdroje Pier (Molo)

Website: n/a
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Molo+Miedzyzdroje
Open: Open access; the resort's #1 landmark, with sweeping Baltic views. Small entry fee in peak season.
Walk: 0.4 km
route in Google Maps
Walk steps: A 5-minute walk down to the seafront and the pier.
Tickets: Free / small seasonal fee.

Promenade & Avenue of Stars (Promenada Gwiazd)

Website: n/a
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Promenada+Gwiazd+Miedzyzdroje
Open: Open access. The lively seafront promenade with handprints of Polish film and music stars — a fun "find the celebrity" game for kids.
Walk: 0.9 km
route in Google Maps
Tickets: Free.

Wax Museum (Gabinet Figur Woskowych)

Website: n/a
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Gabinet+Figur+Woskowych+Miedzyzdroje
Open: Daily, long summer hours (approx. 10:00–21:00). Poland's oldest wax museum — actors, musicians, politicians and film/fairy-tale characters.
Walk: 0.5 km
route in Google Maps
Walk steps: A 6-minute walk along Bohaterów Warszawy to the promenade.
Tickets: ~25–30 PLN adult, reduced for children.

Wolin NP Natural History Museum (Muzeum Przyrodnicze WPN)

Website: https://wolinpn.pl/
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Muzeum+Przyrodnicze+Wolinskiego+Parku+Narodowego+Miedzyzdroje
Open: May–Sep Tue–Sun 09:00–17:00 (tickets to 16:30); CLOSED Mondays. Park ecosystems, birds and animals — a good rainy-hour option.
Walk: 0.8 km
route in Google Maps
Walk steps: A 10-minute walk to Niepodległości 3.
Tickets: ~15 PLN adult, reduced for children.

Międzyzdroje Beach (Plaża)

Website: n/a
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Plaza+Miedzyzdroje
Open: Open access. Wide, clean sandy Baltic beach right below the promenade — buckets, paddling and sandcastles for the 4-year-old.
Walk: 1.5 km
route in Google Maps
Walk steps: Down to the seafront; the beach runs the length of town.
Tickets: Free.

Kawcza Góra Viewpoint

Website: n/a
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Kawcza+Gora+Miedzyzdroje
Open: Open access. A wooded coastal hill at the eastern end of town with a viewing platform over the sea — a short scenic hike in Wolin NP.
Walk: 1.9 km
route in Google Maps
Walk steps: A ~28-minute walk/hike east along the shore and up to the viewpoint.
Tickets: Free.

European Bison Show Reserve (Zagroda Pokazowa Żubrów)

Website: https://wolinpn.pl/
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Zagroda+Pokazowa+Zubrow+Miedzyzdroje
Open: May–Sep daily 10:00–18:00, CLOSED Mondays. See European bison plus eagles and wild boar in a forest enclosure with viewing terraces — a guaranteed hit with kids.
Walk: 3.3 km by car, ~6 min
route in Google Maps
Walk steps: Drive ~3 km along DW102 to the forest car park (or walk ~30 min from town).
Tickets: 6 PLN adult, 4 PLN reduced; under 4 free.

Turquoise Lake (Jezioro Turkusowe), Wapnica

Website: n/a
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Jezioro+Turkusowe+Wapnica
Open: Open access. A striking turquoise lake in a flooded former chalk quarry, with a short forest walk to a viewpoint — beautiful and easy with kids.
Walk: 6.7 km by car, ~10 min
route in Google Maps
Walk steps: Drive ~7 km via DW102 to Wapnica, then a short signed walk to the viewpoint.
Tickets: Free.
