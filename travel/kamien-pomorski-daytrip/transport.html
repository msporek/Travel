Transport

By car — Pobierowo to Kamień Pomorski

Distance: 19 km, ~25 min
driving route in Google Maps
Steps: From Pobierowo via Mickiewicza to DW102, then DW107 toward Kamień Pomorski; into the old town via Lipowa/Gryfitów.
Estimated time: ~25 min
Notes: Park near the Stary Rynek or the marina (paid parking in summer). The old town is then fully walkable.

By car — Kamień Pomorski to Pobierowo

Distance: 19 km, ~27 min
driving route in Google Maps
Steps: Back out via DW107 then DW102, through Dziwnów and the coast road to Pobierowo.
Estimated time: ~27 min

Public transport

Distance: -
Steps: No practical scheduled public-transport route between Pobierowo and Kamień Pomorski appears in journey planners — only sparse regional/PKS buses, often with a change. Driving is strongly recommended for this day trip.
Operating hours: -
Notes: If you must go car-free, check the regional bus operator timetables on the day; allow 1.5–2 h each way.

Parking & Tips

Notes: Aim to arrive before noon for the cathedral and before lunch crowds at the marina. The whole old town (cathedral, square, gate, walls, marina) sits within ~600 m, so one central parking spot covers the day; only Chrząszczewska Island needs the car again.
