Attractions

Konkatedra św. Jana Chrzciciela (Co-Cathedral & Baroque Organ)

Website: https://katedra-kamienpomorski.pl/
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Konkatedra+Kamien+Pomorski
Open: Open to visitors daily outside services. Famous 1669–72 Baroque organ (47 stops). International Organ & Chamber Music Festival: concerts Fridays 19:00, late June–early September.
Walk: 0.4 km
route in Google Maps
Walk steps: A 5-minute stroll from the market square to Plac Katedralny.
Tickets: Church entry free (donation). Organ concert tickets ~25–40 PLN at the door; check the festival programme at festiwalorganowy-kamien.pl.

Marina & Lagoon Promenade

Website: https://marinakamien.pl/
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Marina+Kamien+Pomorski
Open: Open access year-round; liveliest in summer (cafés, boats, ice cream). Passenger boat cruises on the Kamień Lagoon depart here in season.
Walk: 0.1 km
route in Google Maps
Tickets: Free to walk. Lagoon cruise ~25–35 PLN adult, less for children (seasonal; buy at the quay).

Wolin Gate & Museum of Rocks (Brama Wolińska)

Website: n/a
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Brama+Wolinska+Muzeum+Kamien+Pomorski
Open: Medieval brick gate at the western edge of the old town; the 14th-c. tower houses a Museum of Rocks (2,600+ minerals, fossils, dinosaur bones, meteorites). Summer daily approx. 10:00–18:00 (shorter off-season).
Walk: 0.2 km
route in Google Maps
Walk steps: A 3-minute walk west along the old town to the gate.
Tickets: Museum of Rocks ~10–15 PLN adult, reduced for children; under-toddlers free.

route in Google Maps

Gothic Town Hall & Market Square (Ratusz)

Website: n/a
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Ratusz+Kamien+Pomorski
Open: One of the few surviving Gothic town halls in Western Pomerania, standing on the Old Market Square — you're right next to it when you park.
Walk: 0.0 km — on the Stary Rynek itself
route in Google Maps
Tickets: Free to view from the square.

Chrząszczewska Island & the King's Stone (Królewski Głaz)

Website: n/a
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Wyspa+Chrzaszczewska+Krolewski+Glaz
Open: A quiet nature island reached by causeway, with one of Poland's largest glacial erratic boulders — the "King's Stone" — plus lagoon views and birdlife. Open access.
Walk: 5.2 km by car, ~16 min — drive across the causeway
route in Google Maps
Walk steps: Drive ~5 km east via Mieszka I and Orzeszkowej across the causeway to the island.
Tickets: Free. A short, easy add-on if you have the car and an hour to spare.
