Transport

By car — Pobierowo to Świnoujście

Distance: 60 km, ~1 h 3 min
driving route in Google Maps
Steps: DW102 west via Dziwnów and Wolin island, then the E65 expressway through the new Świnoujście tunnel under the Świna onto Uznam and into the town centre.
Estimated time: ~1 h 3 min
Notes: The tunnel (opened 2023) is free and makes the old car ferry unnecessary. Park near Plac Wolności / the spa district.

By car — Świnoujście to Pobierowo

Distance: 60 km, ~1 h 5 min
driving route in Google Maps
Steps: Back through the tunnel onto the E65, then DW102 east via Wolin and Dziwnów to Pobierowo.
Estimated time: ~1 h 5 min

Public transport

Distance: -
Steps: Świnoujście has rail (Szczecin line) and the free city ferries across the Świna, but there is no convenient train/bus link directly from Pobierowo. Driving via the tunnel is by far the simplest.
Operating hours: -
Notes: Within town the free municipal car/passenger ferries connect the Uznam and Warszów banks; the Lighthouse/Fort Gerharda, however, are reached by the separate commercial tour boat.

Parking & Tips

Notes: Book the Lighthouse + Fort Gerharda boat tour early (limited daily departures, busy in summer). Keep the beach, promenade, spa park, fisheries museum and a meal to the walkable Uznam side; the tour covers the rest.
