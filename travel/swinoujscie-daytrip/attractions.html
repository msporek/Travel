Attractions

Świnoujście Lighthouse (Latarnia Morska)

Website: n/a
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Latarnia+Morska+Swinoujscie
Open: Lighthouse museum daily ~10:00–18:00. Poland's tallest lighthouse (and one of the world's) — 280 steps to a panoramic top. Reached via the combined boat tour (below).
Walk: Reached by the combined boat tour from the town-centre quay (boat-only access since the LNG safety zone).
Tickets: Combined boat tour (transfer + lighthouse + Fort Gerharda, guided): ~59 PLN adult, ~34 PLN child. Boats run mainly May–Sep; book at the quay.

Fort Gerharda (Coastal Defence Museum)

Website: http://fort-gerharda.pl/
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Fort+Gerharda+Swinoujscie
Open: One of the best-preserved Prussian forts on the Baltic, with costumed living-history guided tours (and cannon firing in season) — a big hit with kids. Reached via the combined boat tour.
Walk: Reached by the combined boat tour from the town-centre quay (same ticket as the lighthouse).
Tickets: Included in the ~59 PLN adult / ~34 PLN child combined boat-tour ticket.

Stawa Młyny (Harbour Marker)

Website: n/a
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Stawa+Mlyny+Swinoujscie
Open: Open access. The white windmill-shaped navigation marker at the western breakwater — the symbol of Świnoujście and the classic beach photo.
Walk: 3.3 km (~45 min along the beach/promenade)
route in Google Maps
Walk steps: A long but flat walk west along the beach; nicer by bike, or drive to the western beach car park.
Tickets: Free.

Świnoujście Beach (Plaża)

Website: n/a
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Plaza+Swinoujscie
Open: Open access. One of the widest, finest sandy beaches in Poland — endless room for sandcastles and paddling.
Walk: 0.4 km
route in Google Maps
Tickets: Free.

Sea Fisheries Museum (Muzeum Rybołówstwa Morskiego)

Website: https://mrm.swinoujscie.pl/
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Muzeum+Rybolowstwa+Morskiego+Swinoujscie
Open: Tue–Sat 09:00–17:00, Sun 10:00–15:00, CLOSED Mondays. Aquaria, ships and maritime history in the old town hall on Plac Rybaka — a good rainy-hour stop.
Walk: 0.2 km
route in Google Maps
Walk steps: A 3-minute walk to Plac Rybaka 1.
Tickets: ~15 PLN adult, reduced for children.

Harbour Cruise (boat trip)

Website: n/a
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Rejsy+statkiem+Swinoujscie+nabrzeze
Open: Seasonal (May–Sep). ~2-hour pirate-style harbour cruises (e.g. Róża Wenedów) along the Świna and port — a fun, low-effort hit with kids.
Walk: 0.8 km
route in Google Maps
Walk steps: An 11-minute walk to the waterfront quays on Wybrzeże Władysława IV.
Tickets: ~40–60 PLN adult, less for children; buy at the quay.
