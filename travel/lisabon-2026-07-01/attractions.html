Castelo de Sao Jorge

Website: https://castelodesaojorge.pt/en/
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Castelo%20de%20Sao%20Jorge%2C%20Lisboa
Open: Daily 09:00-21:00 (summer Mar-Oct).
Walk: 1.3 km
route in Google Maps
Walk steps: Down R. Garrett, through Baixa via R. da Conceicao, up steep stairs through Alfama (Esc. de S. Crispim), final stretch on R. de Santa Cruz do Castelo. Some stairs - bring stroller-free hands.
Public transit: 1.9 km, ~17 min
route in Google Maps
Transit steps: Walk 7 min to Pc. Figueira, Bus 737 (5 stops, headsign "Castelo") drops you at the gate, 2 min walk uphill.
Tickets: Adult EUR 17 | Youth 13-25 EUR 8.50 | Child <=12 FREE | Senior 65+ EUR 14. Pre-book online via BOL (only authorized seller). Skip-the-line ticket recommended in summer - on-site queues run 30-60 min in July.

Walk: 6.6 km
Walk on Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=R.%20da%20Oliveira%20ao%20Carmo%208%2C%201200-309%20Lisboa%2C%20Portugal&destination=Pra%C3%A7a%20do%20Imp%C3%A9rio%201400-206%20Lisboa%2C%201400-206%20Lisboa%2C%20Portugal&travelmode=walking

route in Google Maps

Walk steps: NOT recommended - take public transit instead.
Public transit: 8.0 km, ~48 min
route in Google Maps
Transit steps: Same as Jeronimos - take Tram 15E from Corpo Santo, 16 stops to Lg. Princesa, then 6 min walk along the riverside to the tower. Pair with Jeronimos in one day; they are 1 km apart along the river.
Tickets: When reopened: Adult ~EUR 8 | Children <14 FREE. Combo with Jeronimos saves EUR 4. Even if interior closed, exterior + riverfront is worth the trip - the tower from across the water is iconic.

Tram 28E (board at Praca Martim Moniz)

Website: https://www.carris.pt/en/travel/carreiras/28e/
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Praca%20Martim%20Moniz%2C%20Lisboa
Open: Daily 06:00-23:00, every 10-15 min. Full route Martim Moniz - Cemiterio dos Prazeres, ~45 min.
Walk: 0.8 km
route in Google Maps
Walk steps: Down through Largo do Carmo, R. Dom Antao de Almada, across Praca da Figueira, into Martim Moniz square. The starting line forms here - earlier the better.
Public transit: 1.4 km, ~9 min
route in Google Maps
Transit steps: Metro Green Line from Baixa-Chiado to Martim Moniz, 2 stops (headsign Telheiras).
Tickets: EUR 1.90 with Viva Viagem card (one-zone Carris/Metro 1-hour ticket). EUR 3.30 onboard with cash. EUR 7.25 24-hour pass = best value if you ride 4+ rides/day. Buy and load Viva Viagem at any metro station vending machine (EUR 0.50 card fee). TIP: Board at Martim Moniz (not Largo do Chiado near hotel) - get a window seat. Travel BEFORE 09:00 or AFTER 19:00 in summer; midday is packed and pickpockets are active.

Time Out Market Lisboa (Mercado da Ribeira)

Website: https://www.timeoutmarket.com/lisboa/
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Time%20Out%20Market%20Lisboa
Open: Sun-Wed 10:00-00:00, Thu-Sat 10:00-02:00. Daily.
Walk: 1.0 km
route in Google Maps
Walk steps: Down R. Trindade, through Largo do Chiado and Praca Luis de Camoes, down R. do Alecrim to the riverside.
Public transit: 1.1 km, ~9 min
route in Google Maps
Transit steps: Metro Green Line from Baixa-Chiado to Cais do Sodre, 1 stop, then 3 min walk.
Tickets: No reservation, no entry fee. ~30 stalls + central food hall. Good kid food (pizza, gelato, hamburgers) plus serious chef stalls (Henrique Sa Pessoa, Marlene Vieira). Communal tables get crowded 13:00-15:00 and 20:00-22:00 - aim for off-peak. Walk back uphill OR take the Bica funicular for fun.

Miradouro de Santa Luzia + Alfama wander

Website: n/a
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Miradouro%20de%20Santa%20Luzia%2C%20Lisboa
Open: Open 24/7. Free.
Walk: 1.4 km
route in Google Maps
Walk steps: Across Baixa via R. da Conceicao, through Lg. da Madalena, past the Se Cathedral, up R. Augusto Rosa, two short stair sections at the end.
Public transit: 1.4 km, ~14 min
route in Google Maps
Transit steps: Walk 7 min to R. Conceicao, then Tram 28E (4 stops, headsign Martim Moniz) to "Miradouro Sta. Luzia". Combine with the Tram 28E ride and you get both.
Tickets: Free. Best at golden hour (~19:00-20:00 in July). Tile-clad terrace with wisteria; Tagus + tile-roof view of Alfama. Continue down through Alfama's narrow lanes for fado bars and pastel-de-nata cafes. Steep cobbles - sandals not flip-flops.

Open: Open 24/7 (individual shops typically 11:00-20:00). LX Market every Sunday 11:00-20:00 (summer).

Jardim do Principe Real

Website: n/a
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Jardim%20do%20Principe%20Real%2C%20Lisboa
Open: Open 24/7. Free.
Walk: 0.9 km
route in Google Maps
Walk steps: Up Calcada do Duque (a few stairs), through Largo Trindade Coelho (Igreja de Sao Roque), up R. de Sao Pedro de Alcantara (passes Jardim Sao Pedro de Alcantara - viewpoint), continue up R. Dom Pedro V to the square.
Public transit: 0.9 km, ~7 min
route in Google Maps

Jardim da Estrela

Website: n/a
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Jardim%20da%20Estrela%2C%20Lisboa
Open: Daily ~07:00-22:00. Free.
Walk: 1.8 km
route in Google Maps
Walk steps: Up via Calcada do Duque to Principe Real, continue west on R. da Escola Politecnica, down R. da Imprensa Nacional, into Jardim via R. de Santo Amaro.
Public transit: 2.0 km, ~20 min
route in Google Maps
Transit steps: Walk 6 min to Pc. Luis Camoes, Tram 28E (8 stops, headsign "Campo Ourique") to Estrela (Basilica). The tram is part of the experience.
Tickets: Free. 4.6 hectares, two playgrounds, animal-shaped slides (giraffe + lion - kid-magnet), sand pit, swings, lake with swans/ducks/peacocks. Kiosk-cafe inside. Best park in central Lisbon for an extended family afternoon.

Jardim do Torel

Website: n/a
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Jardim%20do%20Torel%2C%20Lisboa
Open: Daily ~07:00-22:00. Free. Summer pool typically open Jun 15 - Sep 15, ~10:00-19:00.
Walk: 1.1 km
route in Google Maps
Walk steps: Across Praca dos Restauradores, up R. das Portas de Santo Antao, right onto Calcada do Lavra (steep + stairs), final stretch via Tv. da Cruz do Torel.
Public transit: 1.1 km, ~19 min
Transit on Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=R.%20da%20Oliveira%20ao%20Carmo%208%2C%201200-309%20Lisboa%2C%20Portugal&destination=R.%20J%C3%BAlio%20de%20Andrade%2C%201150-122%20Lisboa%2C%20Portugal&travelmode=transit
Transit steps: No transit route shorter than the walk. Alt: Lavra funicular from Largo da Anunciada to just below Torel (EUR 4 return, ~07:00-21:00) saves the steep climb.
Tickets: Garden free; pool small admission ~EUR 2-4 expected. SHADED PICNIC LAWNS, panoramic views over Restauradores and Castelo. The kid-pool is a real draw on hot July afternoons. Bring towels, swimsuits, snacks. Quiet, locals-mostly atmosphere.
