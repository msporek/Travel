Transport

AIRPORT TO HOTEL

Metro - Red Line then Green Line (recommended)

Distance: ~8 km, ~30 min total
transit route in Google Maps
Steps: Aeroporto metro (Red Line/Vermelha) -> 5 stops to Alameda -> change to Green Line/Verde -> 3 stops to Baixa-Chiado -> 5 min walk uphill via Calcada do Sacramento and Largo do Carmo to hotel.
Ticket: EUR 1.85 single (1.61 with Viva Viagem card). Card costs EUR 0.50 (one-time, valid 1 year). Buy and load at the airport metro station vending machines (English interface).
Operating hours: Daily 06:30 - 01:00
Notes: Cheapest option. Best when arriving with a single suitcase per person. With 4 travelers + 4 large bags + a 4yo, you may prefer a taxi - the Baixa-Chiado station has stairs and the final 5 min walk is uphill on cobbles.

Bus - Carris 722

Distance: ~7.4 km, ~33-40 min depending on traffic
Transit on Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Humberto%20Delgado%20Airport%20(LIS)%2C%20Alameda%20das%20Comunidades%20Portuguesas%2C%201700-111%20Lisboa%2C%20Portugal&destination=Pra%C3%A7a%20dos%20Restauradores%2C%201250-188%20Lisboa%2C%20Portugal&travelmode=transit
Steps: From Aeroporto bus stop, take Bus 722 (headsign Praca de Espanha or Restauradores) to Restauradores - 20 stops, ~30 min. Walk 8 min uphill to hotel. Or stay on for one more stop and walk through Largo do Carmo.
Ticket: EUR 1.85 with Viva Viagem card, EUR 2.10 cash on bus. Same Viva Viagem as the metro - one card works on both.
Operating hours: ~05:30 - 00:30
Notes: Slowest option due to traffic. Useful if metro is closed or if your hotel is on the bus line. Bus 744 also runs Aeroporto - Marques de Pombal - Cais do Sodre.

Taxi / Rideshare (Uber, Bolt, FreeNow)

Distance: 9.8 km, ~24 min driving (without traffic)
driving route in Google Maps
Apps available: Uber: yes | Bolt: yes (often cheapest) | FreeNow: yes (taxi aggregator)
Estimated fare: Bolt EUR 8-15 | Uber EUR 10-18 | Official taxi EUR 12-18 (meter, depending on traffic). Taxi minimum: EUR 3.32. Surcharge ~EUR 1.60 for luggage in trunk.
Estimated time: 20-30 min off-peak, 35-50 min during morning rush (07:30-09:30) or evening rush (17:00-19:30)
Notes: FASTEST AND MOST CONVENIENT for a family of 4 with luggage. Door-to-door, kid-seat available on Uber Comfort. Use Uber/Bolt to lock the price upfront and avoid taxi rank scams (some Lisbon taxi drivers are notorious for "broken meters" or detours). The official taxi rank IS legitimate but Uber/Bolt is usually cheaper and English-friendly. Rideshare pickup zone is signed at Terminal 1 Arrivals - exit and walk to the dedicated zone (NOT the taxi rank).

HOTEL TO AIRPORT

Metro - Green Line then Red Line (recommended)

Distance: ~8 km, ~30 min total
transit route in Google Maps
Steps: Walk 5 min downhill to Baixa-Chiado station -> Green Line/Verde towards Telheiras -> 3 stops to Alameda -> change to Red Line/Vermelha -> 5 stops to Aeroporto. Exit straight into Terminal 1 departures.
Ticket: EUR 1.85 single, or use existing Viva Viagem card.
Operating hours: Daily 06:30 - 01:00
Notes: Best ratio of speed + cost. Allow ~50 min total to airport (5 min walk + 30 min metro + 15 min margin to security). Has stairs at Baixa-Chiado - one of you takes a small bag while the other handles big luggage.

Bus - Carris 722

Distance: ~8.3 km, ~42 min
Steps: Walk 8 min to Restauradores -> Bus 722 (headsign Portela - R. Escritores) -> 20 stops, ~29 min -> Aeroporto (Partidas) -> 5 min walk into Terminal 1.
Ticket: EUR 1.85 with Viva Viagem card
Operating hours: ~05:30 - 00:30
Notes: Direct bus to airport. Slow in traffic. Avoid morning rush.

Taxi / Rideshare

Distance: 7.9 km, ~21 min driving (without traffic)
driving route in Google Maps
Apps available: Uber: yes | Bolt: yes | FreeNow: yes
Estimated fare: Bolt EUR 8-15 | Uber EUR 10-18 | Official taxi EUR 12-18 (meter)
Estimated time: 20-30 min off-peak, 35-50 min in morning rush
Notes: Mon Jul 6 (your departure day) is a weekday - book your Uber/Bolt at least 60 min before you actually need to be at the airport, especially if your flight is mid-morning. The hotel reception can also call a taxi for you (no extra fee). The drop-off at Departures is well organized.
