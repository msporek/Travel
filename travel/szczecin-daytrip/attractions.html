Attractions

Pomeranian Dukes' Castle (Zamek Książąt Pomorskich)

Website: https://zamek.szczecin.pl/
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Zamek+Ksiazat+Pomorskich+Szczecin
Open: Renaissance castle with free courtyards open daily; the bell tower has a viewing terrace and there's a café and exhibitions (ticketed).
Walk: 0.9 km
route in Google Maps
Walk steps: A 12-minute walk north-east from Brama Portowa to the castle.
Tickets: Courtyards free; tower viewpoint ~10–15 PLN; exhibition tickets vary.

Wały Chrobrego (Chrobry Embankment)

Website: n/a
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Waly+Chrobrego+Szczecin
Open: Open access. Grand monumental terraces above the Oder with sweeping views of the river and port — Szczecin's signature promenade; boat trips depart below.
Walk: 1.5 km
route in Google Maps
Tickets: Free.

Szczecin Underground Routes (Trasa Podziemna)

Website: https://schron.szczecin.pl/en/
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Szczecinskie+Podziemne+Trasy+Turystyczne
Open: Guided tours daily 12:00 and 13:30 (extra 10:00 & 11:00 at weekends). Poland's largest civilian WWII shelter, under the main station — a slightly spooky adventure teens love. English tours at set times.
Walk: 0.8 km
route in Google Maps
Walk steps: An 11-minute walk down to the main railway station entrance.
Tickets: ~20–28 PLN per person; book ahead in summer. Not ideal for the 4-year-old (dark/steps) but great for the 13-year-old.

St. James' Cathedral Tower (Bazylika św. Jakuba)

Website: n/a
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Bazylika+sw+Jakuba+Szczecin
Open: Lift to a glassed viewing gallery ~50 m up for a 360° view over the city. Open to visitors outside services.
Walk: 0.5 km
route in Google Maps
Walk steps: An 8-minute walk to the cathedral.
Tickets: Tower lift ~10–15 PLN; church free.

Dialogue Centre Przełomy (Centrum Dialogu Przełomy)

Website: https://przelomy.muzeum.szczecin.pl/
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Centrum+Dialogu+Przelomy+Szczecin
Open: Tue–Sun, CLOSED Mondays. An award-winning sunken-plaza museum of Szczecin's recent (1939–1989) history; the wavy white plaza above is a free public space kids can run on.
Walk: 1.0 km
route in Google Maps
Walk steps: A 14-minute walk to Plac Solidarności.
Tickets: Museum ~ small fee (free day midweek); plaza free.

Eureka Science Centre (Centrum Nauki Eureka)

Website: n/a
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Centrum+Nauki+Eureka+Szczecin
Open: Interactive hands-on science centre (physics, chemistry, biology) at the Maritime University by Wały Chrobrego — check current opening hours before you go.
Walk: 1.4 km
route in Google Maps
Walk steps: A 20-minute walk toward Wały Chrobrego; combine with the embankment.
Tickets: Modest entry fee; great rainy-hour option for both kids.

Odra River Cruise

Website: n/a
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Rejsy+po+Odrze+Waly+Chrobrego+Szczecin
Open: Seasonal (approx. May–Sep). Sightseeing cruises along the Oder and shipyard depart from below Wały Chrobrego — an easy, scenic hit with kids.
Walk: 1.5 km
route in Google Maps
Walk steps: Down to the quay below Wały Chrobrego.
Tickets: ~30–45 PLN adult, reduced for children; buy at the quay.

Museum of Technology & Communication (Muzeum Techniki i Komunikacji)

Website: https://muzeumtechniki.eu/en/
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Muzeum+Techniki+i+Komunikacji+Szczecin
Open: Tue 10:00–15:00, Wed–Fri 10:00–16:00, Sat 10:00–18:00, Sun 10:00–16:00; CLOSED Mondays. Vintage trams, cars, buses and motorbikes in an old tram depot — plus a tram-driver simulator. A top kids' pick.
Walk: 3.0 km
route in Google Maps
Walk steps: Too far to walk comfortably — take the tram.
Public transit: 3.2 km, ~15 min
route in Google Maps
Transit steps: TRAM 3 from Brama Portowa, 8 stops to the "Muzeum Techniki" stop — right at the door.
Tickets: ~15–20 PLN adult, reduced for children; simulator may be extra.

Kasprowicz Park, Różanka & Jasne Błonia

Website: n/a
Google Maps: https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Park+Kasprowicza+Szczecin
Open: Open access. A huge green belt with the Różanka rose garden, the Emerald Lake nearby, playgrounds and the grand Jasne Błonia lawns — room to run and picnic.
Walk: 2.8 km
route in Google Maps
Public transit: 3.7 km, ~24 min
route in Google Maps
Transit steps: TRAM 3 from Brama Portowa toward Muzeum Techniki, then a ~0.6 km walk into the park.
Tickets: Free.
