Transport

By car — Pobierowo to Szczecin

Distance: 104 km, ~1 h 25 min
driving route in Google Maps
Steps: DW102/DW107 inland to the S3 expressway at Parłówko, then S3 → A6 → DK10 into central Szczecin (Brama Portowa).
Estimated time: ~1 h 25 min
Notes: The longest of the five trips. Park in a central garage (e.g. by Kaskada/Brama Portowa); then walk and use trams.

By car — Szczecin to Pobierowo

Distance: 104 km, ~1 h 23 min
driving route in Google Maps
Steps: DK10 → A6 → S3 north, exit at Parłówko onto DW107/DW102 back to the Pobierowo coast.
Estimated time: ~1 h 23 min

Public transport

Distance: -
Steps: There is no convenient direct public-transport link from Pobierowo to Szczecin (you would change via Gryfice/Goleniów by train, ~3 h+). Driving is strongly recommended. Within Szczecin, trams and buses are excellent — a single ZDiTM ticket covers them.
Operating hours: Szczecin trams/buses run roughly 05:00–23:00.
Notes: In the city, buy a time ticket from machines/onboard; TRAM 3 from Brama Portowa serves the Technology Museum and Kasprowicz Park.

Parking & Tips

Notes: Plan the day as a central walking loop (Kaskada → Brama Portowa → cathedral → Przełomy → castle → Wały Chrobrego/Eureka), then one tram ride out to the Technology Museum. Underground Routes run guided tours at fixed times (12:00/13:30) — plan lunch around them.
